# Breathwork for Anxiety 🧘‍♂️

Anxiety can make you feel out of control. One of the quickest ways to calm down is through **controlled breathing**.

## Why Does Breathwork Help?

- Regulates your **nervous system** 🧠
- Increases **oxygen flow** to your brain 💨
- Helps shift from **fight-or-flight** to **rest-and-digest** mode 🏞️

## Try This Simple Exercise

This is a **4-7-8 Breathing** technique:

1. Inhale through your nose for **4 seconds** 🫁
2. Hold your breath for **7 seconds** ⏳
3. Exhale slowly through your mouth for **8 seconds** 💨
4. Repeat **4 times**

## When to Use This

- Before an **important event** (e.g., a presentation) 🎤
- If you wake up feeling **stressed** 😓
- During moments of **panic** 🚨

### Final Thoughts

Breathwork is a **powerful tool** for managing anxiety. Try it out and let us know how it works for you!
